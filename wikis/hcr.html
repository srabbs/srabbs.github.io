Hardcore Radiation

Hardcore Radiation is a management simulator game developed by independent greek game developer srabb. The game's development started in early 2023 and it will make it's first release on itch.io. The game took inspiration from other management simulator games like Prison Architect, RimWorld and Dwarf Fortress. The game even has it's own website and the developer has said that the price of the game will most likely be $3.99 but it has not been decided yet.
